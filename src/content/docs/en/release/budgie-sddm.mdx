Budgie Edition switched to Wayland in Ultramarine 44, meaning it needs a new greeter to work fully.

You may have received a notification about this on your Budgie system that lead you here.

## Switching to SDDM
This part is easy, just run `switch2sddm` in the terminal, you will be prompted for your password twice, after the program finishes you'll need to reboot, so save your work and press enter to do it.
