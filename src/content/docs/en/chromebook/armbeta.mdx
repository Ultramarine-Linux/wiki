NOTE - Not all ARM devices are currently supported. This is a Beta program, proceed at your own risk.

Welcome to the ARM Chromebook Beta Program! We are looking for technically-versed people to test our Ultramarine Chromebook beta images for ARM.

This is a closed program, please join and ping @Owen in the [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/5fdPuxTg5Q) or [Matrix](https://matrix.to/#/#hub:fyralabs.com) (most prefered), or email him at owen@fyralabs.com, to apply.
